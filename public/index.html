⛔ Stop Work Immediately

If you suspect asbestos, stop all work in the affected area immediately.

  1. Seal off the area and prevent access
  2. Inform your customer in writing right now
  3. Do not remove or bag the material yourself
  4. Contact a licensed asbestos surveyor
  5. Document everything with photos
  6. Only return once a clearance certificate is issued
Variation Notice Wording

"Work has been suspended in [area] following discovery of a suspected asbestos-containing material. Under HSE regulations and my duty of care, all work must cease until a licensed surveyor has assessed and cleared the site. This falls outside the scope of our original agreement."
